About Us

Founded in 1943, our humble restaurant started off our business by recycling the vegetables and fruits of local farmers deemed visually unacceptable into delicious food that people could enjoy. With more farmers than ever, a surplus of goods flushed into the market, and not everything could be sold. We took those discarded leftovers to ensure no mouth was left hungy. Since then, we have aimed to provide everyone worldwide the possibility to taste exquisite cuisine from resources that would have gone to waste otherwise, benefiting both the farmers and customers.

Our Goal

At Humble Roots, sustainability is not just a philosophy—it's the foundation of everything we serve. By partnering with farmers across the globe, we rescue produce that would otherwise be discarded due to superficial imperfections, despite being perfectly fresh and flavorful. This commitment allows us to reduce food waste on a global scale while celebrating the natural diversity of ingredients in our thoughtfully crafted dishes. From root to stem, every part of the ingredient is honored, and every plate tells a story of second chances. Humble Roots is proud to redefine what it means to eat sustainably—deliciously, consciously, and with purpose.

Humble Roots has achieved these goals through a dedicated supply network that connects directly with small-scale farmers and cooperatives who share our vision of reducing agricultural waste. By creating long-term partnerships and investing in logistics that prioritize low-impact transportation and ethical sourcing, we ensure that every ingredient has a journey rooted in integrity. Our kitchen practices zero-waste cooking methods, composting, and reusing wherever possible, turning what others discard into culinary creativity. But the mission doesn't stop with us—our guests play a vital role. By choosing to dine with Humble Roots, you're supporting a global movement toward conscious consumption. We encourage you to take these values home: buy "ugly" produce, support local markets, compost, and share the story of sustainable eating. Together, we can create a ripple effect—transforming not just meals, but mindsets.
